Shradha - faith

Shradha is the faith and trust we all should have in our supreme. Faith is that light which keeps us away from the  darkness,Faith is that strong connection which gives us direction.Nothing is impossible when we have faith. The first step in building faith is to surrender. Grace and blessings can only be achieved with true faith.



Seva - selfless service

Humans have been given the greatest energy of performing selfless service to those in need . Without any expectations in return is seva . Seva is the true form of worship.

Our gurusthan's primary goal is selfless service.Seva such as easy ways of meditation to connect the individual with the supreme, fodder to the cows, food for the needy people, Medicine and food for animal shelter, school fees are paid for the children in need are the services  performed by our Gurusthan.


Saburi- Patience

Patience is the key quality that every individual needs to in build in themselves to attain their goals. In today's world we humans are always in a hurry to seek results and draw conclusions without giving anytime and a proper thought. Saburi, the teaching of Sai Baba helps us to stay hopeful and calm even during complex situations.
